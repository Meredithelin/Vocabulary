# CET-6 词汇 - 睡前背诵

## 1. **deteriorate** /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 恶化，变坏
- **例句**: His health began to deteriorate rapidly.

## 2. **advocate** /ˈædvəkeɪt/

- **词性**: v./n.
- **释义**: 提倡，拥护；倡导者
- **例句**: Many experts advocate a reduction in sugar consumption.

## 3. **ambiguous** /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 模棱两可的，含糊不清的
- **例句**: The contract contained several ambiguous clauses.

## 4. **contemplate** /ˈkɒntəmpleɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 沉思，考虑
- **例句**: She contemplated her future career options.

## 5. **elaborate** /ɪˈlæbərət/

- **词性**: adj./v.
- **释义**: 精心制作的；详细阐述
- **例句**: They made elaborate preparations for the ceremony.

## 6. **fluctuate** /ˈflʌktʃueɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 波动，起伏
- **例句**: Prices fluctuate according to supply and demand.

## 7. **legitimate** /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 合法的，正当的
- **例句**: She had a legitimate reason for being late.

## 8. **manipulate** /məˈnɪpjuleɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 操纵，控制
- **例句**: He tried to manipulate public opinion.

## 9. **obsolete** /ˈɒbsəliːt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 过时的，废弃的
- **例句**: This technology is now obsolete.

## 10. **perpetual** /pəˈpetʃuəl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 永久的，持续的
- **例句**: They lived in perpetual fear of discovery.

## 11. **rigorous** /ˈrɪɡərəs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 严格的，严密的
- **例句**: The selection process is rigorous.

## 12. **scrutinize** /ˈskruːtənaɪz/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 仔细检查，审查
- **例句**: The committee will scrutinize the proposal carefully.

## 13. **suppress** /səˈpres/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 镇压，抑制
- **例句**: She tried to suppress her anger.

## 14. **tangible** /ˈtændʒəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 有形的，实际的
- **例句**: We need tangible evidence to support this claim.

## 15. **undermine** /ˌʌndəˈmaɪn/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 削弱，破坏
- **例句**: This could undermine public confidence in the system.

## 16. **versatile** /ˈvɜːsətaɪl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 多才多艺的，通用的
- **例句**: She is a versatile actress who can play any role.

## 17. **vulnerable** /ˈvʌlnərəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 易受伤害的，脆弱的
- **例句**: The system is vulnerable to cyber attacks.

## 18. **withstand** /wɪðˈstænd/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 经受，承受
- **例句**: The bridge can withstand strong winds.

## 19. **acknowledge** /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 承认，认可
- **例句**: He acknowledged his mistake publicly.

## 20. **compensate** /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 补偿，赔偿
- **例句**: The company will compensate victims for their losses.
