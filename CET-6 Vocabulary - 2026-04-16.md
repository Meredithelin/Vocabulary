# CET-6 词汇 - 2026-04-16 睡前背诵

## 1. **apprehensive** /ˌæprɪˈhensɪv/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 忧虑的，担心的
- **例句**: She was apprehensive about the results of the exam.

## 2. **articulate** /ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlɪt/

- **词性**: adj./v.
- **释义**: 表达清楚的；清楚地表达
- **例句**: He is an articulate speaker who can explain complex ideas clearly.

## 3. **aspire** /əˈspaɪər/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 渴望，追求，立志
- **例句**: She aspires to become a respected scientist one day.

## 4. **chronic** /ˈkrɒnɪk/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 慢性的，长期的；根深蒂固的
- **例句**: He has suffered from chronic back pain for years.

## 5. **comprehend** /ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 理解，领会，领悟
- **例句**: It is hard to comprehend the scale of the disaster.

## 6. **contempt** /kənˈtempt/

- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 轻视，蔑视，不屑
- **例句**: She looked at him with complete contempt.

## 7. **convey** /kənˈveɪ/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 传达，表达；运输
- **例句**: Words sometimes fail to convey the depth of one's feelings.

## 8. **depict** /dɪˈpɪkt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 描绘，描述，刻画
- **例句**: The painting depicts a peaceful countryside scene.

## 9. **derive** /dɪˈraɪv/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 获得；源于，来自
- **例句**: She derives great pleasure from reading classic novels.

## 10. **elusive** /ɪˈluːsɪv/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 难以捉摸的，难以理解的；难以实现的
- **例句**: Success can be elusive if you lack patience and perseverance.

## 11. **eloquent** /ˈeləkwənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 雄辩的，有说服力的；善于表达的
- **例句**: Her eloquent speech moved the entire audience to tears.

## 12. **empower** /ɪmˈpaʊər/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 赋予权力，授权；使能够
- **例句**: Education empowers people to change their lives.

## 13. **erode** /ɪˈroʊd/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 侵蚀，腐蚀；逐渐削弱
- **例句**: Constant criticism can erode a person's self-confidence.

## 14. **ethical** /ˈeθɪkl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 道德的，伦理的；合乎道德标准的
- **例句**: Doctors are expected to follow strict ethical guidelines.

## 15. **evaluate** /ɪˈvæljueɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 评估，评价，评定
- **例句**: Teachers evaluate students' progress through regular tests.

## 16. **evolve** /ɪˈvɒlv/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 演变，进化；逐步发展
- **例句**: Technology has evolved rapidly over the past decade.

## 17. **formidable** /ˈfɔːrmɪdəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 令人敬畏的，强大的；艰难的
- **例句**: The team faced a formidable opponent in the final match.

## 18. **frugal** /ˈfruːɡl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 节俭的，俭朴的，朴素的
- **例句**: Living a frugal lifestyle helped her save enough money to travel.

## 19. **gratitude** /ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/

- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 感激，感谢之情
- **例句**: She expressed her gratitude to the teacher who inspired her.

## 20. **hinder** /ˈhɪndər/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 阻碍，妨碍，拖累
- **例句**: A lack of resources can hinder the progress of research.
