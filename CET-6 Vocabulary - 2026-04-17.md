# CET-6 词汇 - 2026-04-17 睡前背诵

## 1. **illuminate** /ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 照亮；阐明，揭示
- **例句**: The discovery helped illuminate the root causes of the disease.

## 2. **impede** /ɪmˈpiːd/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 阻碍，妨碍，阻止
- **例句**: Heavy traffic can impede the delivery of emergency supplies.

## 3. **incentive** /ɪnˈsentɪv/

- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 激励，刺激，动机
- **例句**: Bonuses serve as an incentive for employees to work harder.

## 4. **indispensable** /ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 不可缺少的，必不可少的
- **例句**: A good dictionary is indispensable for language learners.

## 5. **infer** /ɪnˈfɜːr/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 推断，推论，推测
- **例句**: We can infer from his tone that he was deeply upset.

## 6. **inherent** /ɪnˈhɪərənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 固有的，内在的，本来就有的
- **例句**: There are inherent risks in every business venture.

## 7. **initiate** /ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 开始，发起；使加入
- **例句**: The government decided to initiate a new round of educational reform.

## 8. **innovative** /ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 创新的，革新的
- **例句**: The company is known for its innovative approach to product design.

## 9. **integrate** /ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 整合，融合；使一体化
- **例句**: Schools should integrate technology into everyday classroom teaching.

## 10. **integrity** /ɪnˈteɡrəti/

- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 诚实，正直；完整性
- **例句**: Acting with integrity is essential in any professional field.

## 11. **intervene** /ˌɪntərˈviːn/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 干预，介入；调停
- **例句**: The teacher had to intervene to stop the argument between students.

## 12. **leverage** /ˈlevərɪdʒ/

- **词性**: n./v.
- **释义**: 影响力，杠杆作用；利用
- **例句**: The company used its brand leverage to enter new international markets.

## 13. **manifest** /ˈmænɪfest/

- **词性**: v./adj.
- **释义**: 显示，表明；显而易见的
- **例句**: Stress can manifest itself through a variety of physical symptoms.

## 14. **mitigate** /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 减轻，缓和，缓解
- **例句**: Planting more trees can help mitigate the effects of climate change.

## 15. **mundane** /mʌnˈdeɪn/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 平凡的，世俗的；枯燥乏味的
- **例句**: Even mundane daily tasks can teach us important life lessons.

## 16. **obligation** /ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃn/

- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 义务，职责；债务
- **例句**: Every citizen has an obligation to obey the laws of the country.

## 17. **perceive** /pərˈsiːv/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 察觉，感知；认为，理解
- **例句**: Different people often perceive the same event in very different ways.

## 18. **pragmatic** /præɡˈmætɪk/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 务实的，实用的
- **例句**: She took a pragmatic approach to solving the financial problem.

## 19. **prevalent** /ˈprevələnt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 普遍的，流行的，盛行的
- **例句**: Smartphones are prevalent among people of virtually all age groups.

## 20. **profound** /prəˈfaʊnd/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 深刻的，深远的；渊博的
- **例句**: Reading classic literature can have a profound effect on one's thinking.
