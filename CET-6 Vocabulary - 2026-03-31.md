# CET-6 词汇 - 2026-03-31

## 1. **accelerate** /əkˈseləreɪt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 加速，促进
- **例句**: The government plans to accelerate economic reforms.

## 2. **arbitrary** /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 任意的，武断的
- **例句**: The decision seemed arbitrary and unfair.

## 3. **coherent** /kəʊˈhɪərənt/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 连贯的，一致的
- **例句**: She presented a coherent argument.

## 4. **diminish** /dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 减少，降低
- **例句**: The pain will gradually diminish over time.

## 5. **enhance** /ɪnˈhɑːns/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 提高，增强
- **例句**: This software can enhance image quality.

## 6. **feasible** /ˈfiːzəbl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 可行的，可能的
- **例句**: The plan is economically feasible.

## 7. **implement** /ˈɪmplɪment/
- **词性**: v./n.
- **释义**: 实施，执行；工具
- **例句**: The company will implement new policies next month.

## 8. **inevitable** /ɪnˈevɪtəbl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 不可避免的，必然的
- **例句**: Change is inevitable in any organization.

## 9. **justify** /ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 证明...正当，为...辩护
- **例句**: How can you justify spending so much money?

## 10. **magnitude** /ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 巨大，重要性
- **例句**: We didn't realize the magnitude of the problem.

## 11. **negligible** /ˈneɡlɪdʒəbl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 可忽略的，微不足道的
- **例句**: The cost is negligible compared to the benefits.

## 12. **optimistic** /ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 乐观的
- **例句**: She remains optimistic about the future.

## 13. **preliminary** /prɪˈlɪmɪnəri/
- **词性**: adj./n.
- **释义**: 初步的，预备的
- **例句**: These are just preliminary results.

## 14. **rational** /ˈræʃənl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 理性的，合理的
- **例句**: We need to make a rational decision.

## 15. **skeptical** /ˈskeptɪkl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 怀疑的
- **例句**: Many people are skeptical about the new policy.

## 16. **substantial** /səbˈstænʃl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 大量的，实质的
- **例句**: They made substantial progress this year.

## 17. **tedious** /ˈtiːdiəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 冗长乏味的
- **例句**: The work was tedious and repetitive.

## 18. **unanimous** /juˈnænɪməs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 一致同意的
- **例句**: The committee reached a unanimous decision.

## 19. **vigorous** /ˈvɪɡərəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 精力充沛的，有力的
- **例句**: She took vigorous exercise every day.

## 20. **withdraw** /wɪðˈdrɔː/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 撤退，取回
- **例句**: He decided to withdraw from the competition.
