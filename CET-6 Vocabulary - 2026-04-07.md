# CET-6 词汇 - 2026-04-07 睡前背诵

## 1. **concise** /kənˈsaɪs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 简洁的，简明的
- **例句**: Her answer was concise and easy to understand.

## 2. **coherent** /koʊˈhɪrənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 连贯的，条理清楚的
- **例句**: He gave a coherent explanation of the problem.

## 3. **compelling** /kəmˈpelɪŋ/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 引人注目的；令人信服的
- **例句**: She made a compelling argument in class.

## 4. **deduce** /dɪˈduːs/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 推断，演绎出
- **例句**: We can deduce the answer from the given data.

## 5. **deteriorate** /dɪˈtɪriəreɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 恶化，变坏
- **例句**: His health began to deteriorate after the accident.

## 6. **diligent** /ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 勤奋的，刻苦的
- **例句**: She is a diligent student who reviews every day.

## 7. **discrete** /dɪˈskriːt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 分离的，不连续的
- **例句**: The report divides the process into discrete stages.

## 8. **diversify** /daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 使多样化，拓展
- **例句**: They plan to diversify their investment portfolio.

## 9. **enhance** /ɪnˈhæns/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 提高，增强
- **例句**: Daily practice can enhance your speaking skills.

## 10. **explicit** /ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 明确的，清楚的
- **例句**: The teacher gave explicit instructions for the task.

## 11. **feasible** /ˈfiːzəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 可行的，办得到的
- **例句**: This plan is feasible within our budget.

## 12. **fluctuate** /ˈflʌktʃueɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 波动，起伏
- **例句**: Prices tend to fluctuate during the holiday season.

## 13. **foster** /ˈfɑːstər/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 促进，培养
- **例句**: Group projects foster teamwork and communication.

## 14. **inevitably** /ɪnˈevɪtəbli/

- **词性**: adv.
- **释义**: 不可避免地
- **例句**: Mistakes inevitably happen when we learn something new.

## 15. **meticulous** /məˈtɪkjələs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 一丝不苟的，非常细致的
- **例句**: He is meticulous about checking every detail.

## 16. **negotiate** /nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 协商，谈判
- **例句**: They are trying to negotiate a better contract.

## 17. **persistent** /pərˈsɪstənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 坚持不懈的；持续存在的
- **例句**: Her persistent effort finally paid off.

## 18. **plausible** /ˈplɔːzəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 貌似合理的，似乎可信的
- **例句**: His explanation sounds plausible to me.

## 19. **rectify** /ˈrektɪfaɪ/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 纠正，矫正
- **例句**: We need to rectify the error before publishing.

## 20. **vulnerable** /ˈvʌlnərəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 脆弱的，易受伤害的
- **例句**: Young children are vulnerable to online risks.
