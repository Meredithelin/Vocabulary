# CET-6 词汇 - 2026-04-04 睡前背诵

## 1. **abundant** /əˈbʌndənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 大量的，丰盛的，充裕的
- **例句**: The universe is abundant with stars.

## 2. **ambitious** /æmˈbɪʃəs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 雄心勃勃的，有野心的
- **例句**: She has an ambitious plan for her future.

## 3. **brilliant** /ˈbrɪliənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 巧妙的，出色的，才华横溢的；明亮的
- **例句**: Look at the brilliant moon in the night sky.

## 4. **crucial** /ˈkruːʃl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 至关重要的，决定性的
- **例句**: A good sleep is crucial for your health.

## 5. **delicate** /ˈdelɪkət/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 易碎的，脆弱的；精美的，雅致的
- **例句**: She collected some delicate flowers.

## 6. **equivalent** /ɪˈkwɪvələnt/

- **词性**: adj./n.
- **释义**: 相等的，等价的；相等物
- **例句**: Silence is sometimes equivalent to agreement.

## 7. **fascinating** /ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 迷人的，极有吸引力的
- **例句**: This bedtime story is truly fascinating.

## 8. **genuine** /ˈdʒenjuɪn/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 真正的，名副其实的；真诚的
- **例句**: He showed a genuine interest in the book.

## 9. **harmonious** /hɑːrˈmoʊniəs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 和谐的，和睦的，悦耳的
- **例句**: The music created a harmonious atmosphere.

## 10. **indispensable** /ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 必不可少的，不可或缺的
- **例句**: Water is indispensable to life.

## 11. **inhibit** /ɪnˈhɪbɪt/

- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 抑制，阻碍，禁止
- **例句**: Fear can inhibit people from expressing their opinions.

## 12. **lucrative** /ˈluːkrətɪv/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 赚大钱的，获利丰厚的
- **例句**: He turned his hobby into a lucrative business.

## 13. **magnificent** /mæɡˈnɪfɪsnt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 壮丽的，宏伟的，极好的
- **例句**: We enjoyed a magnificent sunset.

## 14. **notorious** /noʊˈtɔːriəs/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 臭名昭著的，声名狼藉的
- **例句**: The area is notorious for its traffic jams.

## 15. **obscure** /əbˈskjʊr/

- **词性**: adj./v.
- **释义**: 晦涩的，默默无闻的；掩盖，使模糊
- **例句**: The details of the event remain obscure.

## 16. **prominent** /ˈprɑːmɪnənt/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 突出的，显著的，杰出的
- **例句**: The star is very prominent in the dark sky.

## 17. **qualitative** /ˈkwɑːlɪteɪtɪv/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 定性的，性质上的
- **例句**: There has been a qualitative improvement in her English.

## 18. **radical** /ˈrædɪkl/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 根本的，彻底的；激进的
- **例句**: They are proposing a radical change to the system.

## 19. **spectacular** /spekˈtækjələr/

- **词性**: adj./n.
- **释义**: 壮观的，引人入胜的；壮观的场面
- **例句**: The fireworks display was spectacular.

## 20. **profound** /prəˈfaʊnd/

- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 深刻的，渊博的，深奥的
- **例句**: The book had a profound impact on my life.
